The 6x12 Tail Wind series Enclosed Trailer with Tandem Axle offers the high quality cargo transportation capabilities found with the single axle model, but with added stability and payload capability. Tandem axles provide the opportunity for a straighter tow, but for smaller sized cargo trailer like the 6x14, the choice to go tandem or single is mainly a matter of preference and load requirements.

A Trailer Customized to Your Specifications

The 6x14 Enclosed Trailer with Tandem Axle comes with a long list of great standard features, which means you'll be getting more for your money. Nevertheless, there are certain features that do not come standard on the 6x14 Tandem Axle Trailer, but can be easily added for a small fee. Whether you want a customized color for the trailer's exterior, upgraded tires, a reinforced ramp door or any other special features, TrailerShowroom.com is happy to create a trailer that is tailor-made for you. To see our complete menu of options, check out the Enclosed Trailer Master Options List (http://www.trailershowroom.com/master_enclosed_options_txctlcgo.htm).

A Note about Ordering

When ordering your 6x12 Tandem Axle Enclosed Trailer from our Waco, TX facility, you will be only be charged the required deposit amount. The remainder of your bill will be due at the time of pick up. (Price is the total cost for the base trailer or options. Deposits are reflected in the Deposit column. Your balance will equal the total Price less the Deposit.)
