All Aluminum Cargo Trailers

This page features our All Aluminum Elite Trailer selection.  You may also browse our Standard Steel Frame/Aluminum Skin Enclosed Trailers to find lower cost but still high quality cargo trailers.

All aluminum cargo trailers from the Trailer Showroom are built from lightweight and sturdy materials to ensure that your trailer is equipped to handle your most valuable cargo. 

Benefits of choosing an All Aluminum Trailer include:
  • Lightweight - allows you to haul heavier equipment or cargo than standard steel frame trailers
  • Durable - more corrosion resistant than steel frame trailers and ideal for rainy or humid climates
  • Racer Quality - built with style and options to accommodate your most valuable race car

Choose your trailer here and then select your desired options from our list of         aluminum trailer options to customize your trailer.

Our selection of all aluminum enclosed trailers is currently expanding to meet the needs and desires of our clientele so be sure to contact the Trailer Showroom if you can't find what you're looking for here. We also offer a wide selection of steel frame with aluminum skin enclosed cargo trailers.

At The Trailer Showroom, each enclosed all aluminum cargo trailer is custom built to your specifications right in Elkhart, Indiana. Click on any product description below to see a list of available options for each enclosed cargo trailer size.
