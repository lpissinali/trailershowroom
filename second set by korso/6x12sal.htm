Our 6x12 enclosed trailer is ideal as a cargo, utility, or equipment trailer.  This 6 x 12 single axle trailer is custom built for your needs, made in the USA from the highest quality materials, and sold at a price that fits your budget.  Plus, the 6x12 enclosed trailer comes with a 5 year Manufacturer's Limited Warranty!

If the 6x12 enclosed trailer is not the exact size you need, then be sure to shop our huge selection of enclosed cargo trailers, available with a wide range of enclosed trailer options, or check out our selection of open deck trailers.

All 6 x 12 ft enclosed trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer trailer deposits are received.

Note: When ordering, you will be charged only the deposit only.  The balance will not be due until you pick up your finished 6x12 single axle trailer. ( Price is the total cost for the base trailer and options.  Deposits are reflected in the Deposit column.  Your balance due will equal the total Price minus the total Deposit. )

White is our standard trailer color. Many other trailer colors are available as optional equipment. You may view other trailer color swatches on our enclosed trailer color options page.
