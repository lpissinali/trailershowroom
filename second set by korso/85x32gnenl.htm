8.5X32 Gooseneck Enclosed Trailers


The 8.5 x 32' Gooseneck Car Hauler is a durable, dependable, and sleek car trailer worthy of your most prized possession. Customize your own decked out car trailer to accommodate your race car, antique auto, or vintage sports car. We offer tons of options beyond the standard package and encourage you to pick and choose the options that will make your trailer unique.


We understand that you have to trust your trailer to perform in all conditions and under any circumstances. We custom build every trailer to order using high quality materials (we also offer this trailer in an all aluminum version: 8.5 x 32 Gooseneck Aluminum Trailer) and we've earned a respected reputation in the auto community for our products, our value, and our service.


Contact the Trailer Showroom about the 8.5 x 32' Gooseneck Enclosed Trailer by email: askus@trailershowroom.com or call today: 1-877-45-TRAILER.


All 8.5 x 32 ft enclosed trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer trailer deposits are received.

Note: When ordering, you will be charged the deposit only.  The balance will be due when you pick up your finished 8.5 x 32 ft enclosed gooseneck or fifth wheel ( 5th wheel ) car hauler trailer.  ( Price is the total cost for the base trailer or options.  Deposits are reflected in the Deposit column.  Your balance due will equal the total Price minus the total Deposit.)

White is our standard trailer color, but you may select from our enclosed trailer color options for an additional small fee:  Black, Arizona Beige,  Dove Grey, Medium Charcoal Grey, Dark Grey, Silver Frost, Crystal White, Brandywine, Victory Red, Snyder Orange, Emerald Green, Cargo Green, Deep Blue, Indigo Blue, Light Pewter, and Penske Yellow.  See the Exterior Options listed below.
