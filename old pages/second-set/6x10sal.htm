Purchase a 6x10 enclosed trailer custom built to your specifications from the Trailer Showroom.  Browse our master list of enclosed trailer options to take our standard 6x10 enclosed trailer and customize it to suit your specific needs.  6x10 trailers are ideal for a variety of functions including utility and cargo trailers.

If the 6x10 enclosed trailer is not the perfect size for your needs, then we invite you to search our wide selection of enclosed cargo trailers and open deck trailers.  The Trailer Showroom custom manufactures cargo trailers to each particular order right here in the USA.  Enclosed trailers are available for pick up in Douglas, GA or delivery is available (See Buying From Us page for delivery rates).

 

All 6 x 10 ft enclosed trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer trailer deposits are received.

Note: When ordering a 6x10 enclosed trailer, you will be charged the deposit only.  The balance will be due when you pick up your finished trailer.  (Price is the total cost for the base trailer or options.  Deposits are reflected in the Deposit column.  Your balance due will equal the total Price minus the total Deposit.)

White is our standard trailer color. Many other trailer colors are available as optional equipment. You may view other trailer color swatches on our enclosed trailer color options page.
