The 7x20 enclosed trailer is ideal for so many cargo purposes. If you're in the market for a 7x20 cargo trailer, you've come to the right place! We make it easy to design and order your custom cargo trailer online - and at discount trailer prices!

Simply add this 7x20 enclosed trailer to your shopping cart (see link below), look over the standard equipment on all 7 ft trailers, and then choose any trailer options and accessories you want to add, including changes to the structure, tires and wheels, doors and windows, electrical, exterior, etc. You will pay only for the options you choose.

Note: When ordering, you will be charged the deposit only. The balance will be due when you pick up your finished 7x20 enclosed trailer. (Price is the total cost for the base trailer or options. Deposits are reflected in the Deposit column. Your balance due will equal the total Price minus the total Deposit.)

White is our standard trailer color. Many other trailer colors are available as optional equipment. You may view other trailer color swatches on our enclosed trailer color options page.
