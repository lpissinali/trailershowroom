8.5X34 Gooseneck Enclosed Trailers

 

You'll find that the 8.5 x 34' Enclosed Gooseneck Trailer is ideal for use as a car trailer or toy hauler. Durable, high performance, and completely customizable, the 8.5 x 34' car trailer can be trusted with your most prized possession- all at a price that fits your budget!

 

The Trailer Showroom specializes in cargo trailers and enclosed trailers - each one custom built to order. We've developed a reputation based on outstanding products at competitive prices and with excellent customer service. Plus, our company is unique in that we let the customer pick and choose from among hundreds of options to truly customize their trailer.

 

Please contact The Trailer Showroom today for more details about the 8.5 x 34' Gooseneck Enclosed Trailer: 1-877-45-TRAILER.

 

All 8.5 x 34 ft enclosed trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer trailer deposits are received.

Note: When ordering, you will be charged the deposit only.  The balance will be due when you pick up your finished 8.5 x 34 ft enclosed gooseneck or fifth wheel ( 5th wheel ) car hauler trailer.  ( Price is the total cost for the base trailer or options.  Deposits are reflected in the Deposit column.  Your balance due will equal the total Price minus the total Deposit.)

White is our standard trailer color, but you may select from our enclosed trailer color options for an additional small fee:  Black, Arizona Beige,  Dove Grey, Medium Charcoal Grey, Dark Grey, Silver Frost, Crystal White, Brandywine, Victory Red, Snyder Orange, Emerald Green, Cargo Green, Deep Blue, Indigo Blue, Light Pewter, and Penske Yellow.  See the Exterior Options listed below.
