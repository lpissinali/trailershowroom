8.5X28 All Aluminum Gooseneck Trailers

 

Renegade Series

 

 

 

Picture shown with several pieces of optional equipment

 

 

 

Our 8.5x28 enclosed aluminum gooseneck trailer is ideal for use as a car hauler or toy hauler.  All of our trailers are custom built, made from the highest quality materials, and sold at a price that fits your budget. 

If the 8.5 x 28 aluminum gooseneck enclosed trailer is not the exact size you're shopping for, then please check out our huge selection of steel framed enclosed cargo trailers, available in various sizes and styles, and then choose from our complete list of steel framed enclosed trailer options.

All 8.5 x 28 ft enclosed trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer trailer deposits are received.

Note: When ordering, you will be charged the deposit only.  The balance is not due until you pick up your finished 8.5x28 aluminum gooseneck enclosed trailer.  ( Price is the total cost for the base trailer and options.  Deposits are reflected in the Deposit column.  Your balance due will equal the total Price minus the total Deposit.)
