The 10 Ton Tandem Dually Gooseneck Deck Over Trailer is our heavy duty equipment trailer available for order and pick up from our Baxley, GA manufacturing facility.  Please allow a minimum of 2-5 weeks from receipt of your payment for delivery / pick up.  Please see the tables below for exact sizing, pricing, standard and optional equipment available with this trailer.

 

Black is our standard color, but you can choose from a variety of other colors.  See the color choices here and make your selection from the options below.
