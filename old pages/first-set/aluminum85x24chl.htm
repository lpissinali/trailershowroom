Our 8.5x24 enclosed aluminum trailer is ideal for use as a car hauler or toy hauler.  All of our trailers are custom built, made from the highest quality materials, and sold at a price that fits your budget. 

If the 8.5 x 24 aluminum enclosed trailer is not the exact type that you're shopping for, then please check out our huge selection of steel framed enclosed cargo trailers, available in various sizes and styles, and then choose from our complete list of steel framed enclosed trailer options.

All 8.5 x 24 ft enclosed trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer trailer deposits are received.
