Renegade Series

Shown With Optional Cast Corners, Stainless Steel Wraps, Electric Tongue Jack

Our 8.5x16 enclosed aluminum trailer is ideal for use as a car hauler or toy hauler. All of our trailers are custom built, made from the highest quality materials, and sold at a price that fits your budget.

If the 8.5 x 16 aluminum enclosed trailer is not the exact type that you're shopping for, then please check out our huge selection of steel framed enclosed cargo trailers, available in various sizes and styles, and then choose from our complete list of steel framed enclosed trailer options.

All 8.5 x 16 ft enclosed trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer trailer deposits are received.

Note: When ordering, you will be charged the deposit only. The balance is not due until you pick up your finished 8.5x16 aluminum enclosed trailer.
