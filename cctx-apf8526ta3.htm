Our 8.5x26 Auto Plus series enclosed trailer is available with a large variety of options to make sure the trailer is custom made to your specifications. If you're in need of a smaller sized car trailer, then the 8.5x26 tandem axle enclosed car trailer is perfect for you!

If the 8.5x26 Auto Plus series enclosed trailer is not the right size for you, then please shop our selection of enclosed cargo trailers and we're sure you'll find a trailer that's just right for you. We also offer a selection of open deck trailers. All of our cargo trailers are custom made here in the USA and are available for pick up or delivery in select areas.

Note: When ordering, you will be charged the deposit only. The balance will be due when you pick up your finished 8.5x26 tandem axle car trailer. The Price is the total cost for the base trailer or options. Deposits are shown in the Deposit column. Balance due equals the total Price minus the total Deposit.

White is our standard trailer color, but you may select from our enclosed trailer color options for an additional small fee.
