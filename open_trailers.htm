Open Deck Trailers And Optional Equipment

Including Fifth Wheel and Gooseneck Trailers

The Trailer Showroom features an extensive Open Deck Trailer selection in a variety of sizes and styles. Whether you need a landscape utility trailer, a gooseneck deck over trailer, or a heavy equipment trailer, The Trailer Showroom has the open deck trailer you need. Choose from our 5th wheel trailer and gooseneck trailer options plus everything from a tilt tandem axle car hauler to a single axle open deck trailer.

At The Trailer Show Room, each 5th Wheel, Gooseneck, Deck Over, and Open Deck Trailer is custom built in Baxley, Georgia using the highest quality parts and materials.

Please allow a minimum of 2-5 weeks from receipt of your payment for open deck trailer delivery / pick up. All fifth wheel, gooseneck, utility, and open deck trailers are manufactured in the order in which customer payments are received. Manufacture times generally run between 2 and 4 weeks, but could be a little less or a little more depending on our work load/back log at the time that your order is placed.
